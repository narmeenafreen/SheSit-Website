About Us

A Café & Dhaba Designed by Women, for Women

Creating safe spaces and empowering women through food, community, and opportunity.

At SheSit Café & Dhaba, we blend the comfort of a modern café with the warmth of a traditional dhaba. Our mission is to provide women a safe place to connect, work, and relax — while giving aspiring home chefs the platform to share their flavors with the world. Every dish tells a story of community, empowerment, and togetherness.
